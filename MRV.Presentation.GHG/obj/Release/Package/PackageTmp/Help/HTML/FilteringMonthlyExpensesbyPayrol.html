Filtering monthly expenses by payroll items


You can view a particular payroll item's monthly expense using this feature of the system. For instance, managers and other decision makers are usually interested in knowing the total paid amount of a transport allowance for a given month.


Steps

Step 1: Click on the Reports menu -> Click on the Additions\Deductions sub menu. Then a new page that allows users to select period and filter employees based on different criteria will be displayed.

Step 4: Select period and Click on the preview button.
